Overview


This year, our project involved the creation of a wide variety of genetic parts, which served a plethora of purposes. For both the senders' and the receivers' genetic circuits, our team designed new basic parts but also new composite parts. Furthermore, we created a collection of synthetic RBSs, since they were a big part of our project, which we hope can be an essential contribution to future teams. Our collection also includes some pre-existing parts that can already be found in the registry as well as plasmids and primers necessary for our experiments. Finally, we have added the original part we improved, the improved part and all the other parts used in the process of the improvement.
