Protocols

During our working days in the laboratory, we performed different procedures and experiments. From how to prepare simple solutions to more complex experiments, such as the inductions of the Senders and Receivers, we followed certain protocols. All these protocols, that our experiments were based on, can be found below in the Protocols book below (pdf form).
